Charlotte Powning

I'm a UI/UX and visual designer based in Providence, RI. I bring an academic background in anthropology, and have a nuanced and diverse approach to UX research. My professional experience began in education, where I worked with children of all ages both internationally (Japan) and in local communities. I have also taught Japanese at the high school level, and am an illustrator.

My design career has been facilitated through the intersection of my front end development capabilities, my affinity for the visual arts, and my research background. In the visual design space, I enjoy applying elements of balance, color theory, and branding to my work. Outside of work, I enjoy fashion, fitness, and live music.
